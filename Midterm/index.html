Aries

March 21 - April 19
Fire

Energetic and confident, Aries is a natural leader. They are courageous, determined, and full of initiative. Aries can be impulsive and quick-tempered but is always ready to take on new challenges and push boundaries.

Strengths: Courageous, confident, enthusiastic, passionate

Weaknesses: Impulsive, impatient, short-tempered

Soulmate: Leo or Sagittarius

Taurus

April 20 - May 20
Earth

Practical and reliable, Taurus values security and comfort. Patient, determined, and sensual with a love for life's pleasures. Stubborn at times but incredibly loyal and steadfast in relationships.

Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle

Weaknesses: Escapist, overly trusting, idealistic

Soulmate: Cancer or Scorpio

Gemini

May 21 - June 20
Air

Adaptable and curious, Gemini is known for its lively personality and intellectual wit. They love socializing and have a strong need for variety and excitement. However, they can be indecisive and inconsistent at times.

Strengths: Adaptable, witty, outgoing, intellectual

Weaknesses: Indecisive, superficial, restless

Soulmate: Libra or Aquarius

Cancer

June 21 - July 22
Water

Sensitive and caring, Cancer is deeply connected to their emotions and loved ones. They are intuitive, compassionate, and protective, but their moodiness can sometimes be a challenge. Family and home are of utmost importance to them.

Strengths: Compassionate, loyal, intuitive, nurturing

Weaknesses: Moody, insecure, easily hurt

Soulmate: Taurus or Pisces

Leo

July 23 - August 22
Fire

Charismatic and bold, Leo loves being the center of attention. They are generous, creative, and full of confidence. While they have a natural ability to lead, their pride and need for validation can sometimes get in the way.

Strengths: Confident, loyal, creative, passionate

Weaknesses: Arrogant, self-centered, stubborn

Soulmate: Aries or Sagittarius

Virgo

August 23 - September 22
Earth

Practical and detail-oriented, Virgo has a sharp mind and an analytical approach to life. They are hardworking, reliable, and often perfectionistic. Virgo can be overly critical of themselves and others but is always striving for improvement.

Strengths: Analytical, practical, reliable, modest

Weaknesses: Overcritical, perfectionistic, anxious

Soulmate: Taurus or Capricorn

Libra

September 23 - October 22
Air

Charming and diplomatic, Libra is a lover of peace and balance. They are social, cooperative, and have a natural sense of fairness. At times, they can struggle with indecisiveness and can be overly reliant on the opinions of others.

Strengths: Charming, diplomatic, fair, sociable

Weaknesses: Indecisive, superficial, people-pleasing

Soulmate: Gemini or Aquarius

Scorpio

October 23 - November 21
Water

Intense and powerful, Scorpio has a magnetic personality and a deep passion for everything they do. They are courageous, determined, and resourceful. However, their secretive nature and tendency to hold grudges can create challenges.

Strengths: Determined, passionate, resourceful, brave

Weaknesses: Secretive, vengeful, possessive

Soulmate: Cancer or Pisces

Sagittarius

November 22 - December 21
Fire

Optimistic and adventurous, Sagittarius loves exploring new ideas, places, and philosophies. They are independent, honest, and full of enthusiasm. However, their love for freedom can make them commitment-shy, and they can be tactless at times.

Strengths: Adventurous, optimistic, independent, honest

Weaknesses: Tactless, restless, commitment-phobic

Soulmate: Aries or Leo

Capricorn

December 22 - January 19
Earth

Ambitious and disciplined, Capricorn is driven to achieve their goals and succeed. They are practical, patient, and have a strong sense of responsibility. While they can be reserved and serious, they are also loyal and dependable.

Strengths: Ambitious, disciplined, practical, reliable

Weaknesses: Pessimistic, stubborn, workaholic

Soulmate: Taurus or Virgo

Aquarius

January 20 - February 18
Air

Innovative and intellectual, Aquarius is a forward-thinker with a love for originality. They are independent, humanitarian, and often ahead of their time. However, they can be aloof and detached, sometimes making it hard to connect with others emotionally.

Strengths: Innovative, intellectual, independent, humanitarian

Weaknesses: Aloof, unpredictable, detached

Soulmate: Gemini or Libra

Pisces

February 19 - March 20
Water

Compassionate and imaginative, Pisces is a deeply emotional and artistic sign. They are intuitive, empathetic, and gentle. However, their tendency to daydream and avoid reality can sometimes lead them to be overly idealistic or escapist.

Strengths: Compassionate, imaginative, intuitive, empathetic

Weaknesses: Overly idealistic, escapist, overly trusting

Soulmate: Cancer or Scorpio
